URL Shortener

Hey! This is a URL shortener service.


To shorten a long URL given by the string long_url, go to ./encode?url=long_url

Example: http://127.0.0.1:7777/encode?url=https://wikipedia.org


To decode a shortened URL given by the string short_url to its original URL, go to ./decode?url=short_url

Example: http://127.0.0.1:7777/decode?url=http://127.0.0.1:7777/u1U5N
